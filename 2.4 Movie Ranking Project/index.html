The Best Movies According to Doaa

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Home Alone

Eight-year-old Kevin is accidentally left behind when his family leaves for France. At first, he's happy to be in charge; but when thieves try to break into his home, he puts up a fight like no other.

Cats & Dogs

A look at the top-secret, high-tech espionage war going on between cats and dogs, of which their human owners are blissfully unaware.

One Hundred and One Dalmatians

Evil heiress Cruella de Vil decides to make a fur coat for herself out of the skins of Dalmatian puppies. When she steals a litter of 15 puppies, it is up to their parents to rescue the innocent pups.
